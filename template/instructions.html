🧭 How to Edit Headings Animations

1. Hero Heading Animation (Home)

In the homepage hero section, the title is split into multiple lines — for example:SMART / FINANCIAL / MOVES.

Each line has its own animation with a small delay to create a staggered effect:

  • Heading One starts first.
  • Heading Two starts after a short delay.
  • Heading Three starts last.

If your hero title only has two words, just use Heading One and Heading Two animations.
You can see or adjust these inside the Interactions panel on the right side of Webflow.

2. Headings in Other Sections

In other sections (like REAL-TIME FINANCIAL ANALYTICS), the heading animation is different — it uses only one animation that triggers as you scroll.You don’t need to adjust any delays here.Just select the Heading Component and replace the text as shown in the example.
